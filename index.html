Hurry up, Honeybee!

Keep on track every morning, and feel ready for your day.

Always running late?

Despite your best efforts, finding yourself running out the door in a frenzy?



Hurrybee will keep you on time!

After pre-selecting everything you want to accomplish in the morning, Honeybee's timer will count you down through each activity. Never leave home unprepared or late again!





How It Works


  • Set Up Your Morning

    Use an easy drag-and-drop interface to plan out your morning.

  • Race the Clock

    Start your morning and try to finish your activities on time. The app will let you know if you have time to spare, or if you need to pick up the pace! Check off each task as you complete them.

  • Challenges

    Level up your morning by completing challenges to better your health and mood! Challenges do not add time to your tally.

  • Results

    Check how well you did, and learn how to improve next time!
